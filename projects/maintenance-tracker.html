Maintenance Request Tracking System

A workflow automation system that converts spreadsheet-based maintenance request intake into structured tracking, weekly operational summaries, and dashboard-ready outputs.

Business Problem

Maintenance processes often become fragmented across emails, spreadsheets, verbal follow-ups, and manually assembled weekly reports.

This creates poor visibility, inconsistent tracking, and operational friction for teams managing properties or facilities.

System Built

Lionyx-E designed a maintenance tracking workflow that captures request intake through a familiar spreadsheet structure, processes the data through Python, and produces structured tracking and summary outputs.

Request Intake Workbook ↓ Validation & Processing Layer ↓ Python Tracking Engine ↓ Maintenance Dataset ↓ Weekly Summary Output ↓ Operations Dashboard

Core Workflow

The system organizes maintenance information into a repeatable structure so requests can be tracked, summarized, and reviewed without constant manual reshaping of spreadsheet data.

Operational Outputs

Weekly summaries and dashboard-ready outputs improve visibility for managers who need a cleaner picture of maintenance activity and workload distribution.

Business Outcome

The result is a more controlled maintenance workflow, less manual reporting effort, and clearer operational communication.

Technology Structure

This system follows the same Lionyx-E operating model:

  • Excel for practical request intake and visibility
  • Python for workflow processing and structured output generation
  • Summary outputs that support recurring operational reporting
  • A design suitable for admin-heavy property or facilities workflows
